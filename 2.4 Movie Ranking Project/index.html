The Best Movies According to Angela

My Top 3 movies of all-time

The Dark Knight

An inspiring movie with a lot of sci fi to our brain and all the same time
helping us understand why it is always right to be on the good side.

The Godfather

Great film, one of the greatest films of all time for everyone
A cult classic which paved a way for many movies.

About Time

A sci fi romantic teaching us the true meaning of happiness
and why being happy is important.
